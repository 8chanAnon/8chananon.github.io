Before we start, I want to offer some hints toward understanding how to read these posts. 4chan and 8chan are very similar. Posters are normally anonymous and can only be identified by an ID code which persists on one conversation thread only. This makes it impossible to match posters on one thread with those on another. The ID is derived from the poster's IP address and is thus only persistent as long as the IP remains unchanged. This is an issue for users with a dynamic IP since the ID is sometimes useful for tracking a conversation. Depending on the service provider, an IP address may persist for only a few days or for as long as several weeks and it may even change abruptly while it is in use. For that reason, do not be surprised when you see a contributor within a thread suddenly vanish. That person might still be there under another ID.

Originally, Q remained anonymous so the only way to identify who was Q on successive threads was to examine the content and writing style of the posts. Q's style is formal and he seldom references another poster or post directly. That makes it pretty easy to identify Q since most people employ a very casual (and often caustic) style of speech and they frivolously respond to other posters. Q began using what is called a tripcode on November 9. A tripcode is simply an unique ID secured by a password. It is not used to log in or to identify oneself to the system. The purpose is to have an ID that is persistent across threads. There are two levels of tripcode security and Q only used the weak version initially, on purpose, as a honeypot to entrap hackers attempting to obstruct Q's mission.

Every post has a number which is unique to the entire board and is not tied to a particular thread. It is impossible to tell, by its number, what thread a post appears in. This creates some issues which even plague 8chan today since it is possible to reply to a post in another thread but without alerting the unwary viewer that going to that post requires loading up the entirety of the other thread. Perhaps this will be addressed at some time in the future but, for now, one must be aware of impulsive clicking since this can lead to a long wait as the other thread loads. So keep this in mind whenever you visit 4chan or 8chan.

There are a number of sites which track all of Q's posts and qmap.pub is the one which will be referenced here and in subsequent documents. The Q posts shown here all have a link to its entry on qmap.pub where more information is available.
